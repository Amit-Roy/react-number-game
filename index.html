Try to use up all numbers.


1. Select numbers from number panel.
2. Click '=' to verify if your sum equals stars on the left.
3. If sum is correct, '=' will change to a green tick.
4. Click the green tick again to accept the answer.
5. If sum is wrong the '=' will change to a red cross.
6. Click selected numbers to deselect them.
7. Once accepted, the numbers cannot be used again.
8. Exhaust all numbers to win the game.
